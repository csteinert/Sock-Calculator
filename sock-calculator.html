Here's your pattern, Homes.

  1. Cast on stitches. Separate the stitches as evenly as possible onto three needles.
  2. Join, taking care not to twist the work.
  3. Work cuff in pattern for rows.
  4. Work leg in pattern for rows.
  5. At the beginning of the next row, separate the next stitches onto one needle.
  6. Work the separated stitches in a s1k1 pattern.
  7. Turn work, s1 and purl to end of row.
  8. Repeat steps five and six for rows.
  9. Knit the next row for stitches.
  10. Knit the next 2 stitches together, turn work.
  11. Purl stitches, p2tog, turn work.
  12. Knit stitches, k2tog, turn work.
  13. Repeat the last two rows until all stitches have been worked. stitches should remain.
  14. With RS facing you, pick up and knit stitches along the side of the heel flap. Knit the stitches on needle 2 (?). Pick up and knit stitches from the remaining side of the heel flap. Knit the heel stitches. ( stitches)
  15. Knit the next round.
  16. Decrease by 2 stitches every other row for rows. ( stitches)
  17. Knit for rows.
  18. Knit the toe.
